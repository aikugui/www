Requirements
General Requirements
  • non-Chinese citizens with a valid ordinary-type passport, in good health
  • Undergraduate programs: Above 18 years old, high school graduate or above
  • Master’s programs: no more than 35 years old, have attained the Bachelor’s degree.
  • PhD’s programs: no more than 35 years old, have attained the Master’s degree.
  • Note:
  • 1. Graduates who did not receive their degree certificates at the time of application should provide pre-graduation certificates issued by their schools or colleges to prove their ability to graduate smoothly and obtain their degree certificates by July 2020.
  • 2. According to the regulations of the Ministry of Education of China, those who immigrated to other countries from the Mainland China, Hong Kong, Macao or Taiwan and applying to study in Shanghai University as international students, should meet the following additional requirements: the applicants must hold valid passport or other relevant international recognized documents for at least 4 years; meanwhile, in the latest 4 years (by each April 30th of the year of University registration), the applicants must have lived overseas for 2 years (Living overseas for 9 months can be converted to 1 year, which is subject to the exit-entry stamp on visa.).
Language Requirements

1. If you are applying for Chinese-taught programs,

  • HSK certificates required: HSK 4 above 180 for Art;
  • HSK certificates required: HSK 4 above 180 for Art;
  • HSK 4 above 210 for Economics or Management;
  • HSK 5 above 180 for Humanities.

(Note: HSK refers to Chinese Proficiency Test. You can find information on: www.chinesetest.cn) HSK Exemptions The following types of applicants are exempt from taking the HSK: Native speaker of Chinese; The previous degree is fully taught in Chinese.

2. If you are applying for English-taught programs:

  • IELTS 6.0 or TOEFL IBT 80 or above received within the recent two years.
  • TOEFL or IELTS Exemptions The following types of applicants are exempt from taking the TOEFL or IELTS: Native speakers of English; The previous degree is fully taught in English.
